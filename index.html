Join Our Esports Team Too!

Looking for a fun and competitive community to join in the world of esports? Come check out JGT Esports!

We are a growing rocket league team, offering competitive 1v1, 2v2, and 3v3 teams, for players ranked Platinum, to Grand Champ 3, to play in leagues and tournaments.

Our community is dedicated to helping you improve your skills while still having a great time. We are divided into four different groups based on your specific skill set.

Team Fusion based on mechanics,

Team Genesis based on rotations and team play,

Team Impact based on timing your offense and defense,

Team Dauntless based on tying on all three categories together

And its not all about competition either. We want to bring the everyone together. Offering different community events like movie nights, watch parties, game nights, and more. Come hang out with us, make some new friends, and have a blast!

Join JGT Esports today and become part of a awesome and exciting community that's dedicated to helping YOU achieve your esports goals.
